DIU

This post is written by Mosaraf Hossain

Daffodil International University (DIU; Bengali: ড্যাফোডিল আন্তর্জাতিক বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়) is a private research university located in Ashulia, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was established on 24 January 2002 under the Private University Act of 1992 which was replaced by Private University Act 2010.[2] According to QS University Rankings: Asia 2021, DIU is a top-ranked private university among all the universities in Bangladesh.[3] DIU placed 1st among universities ranked from Bangladesh in Times Higher Education Impact Rankings 2021.[4] DIU is the first university in Bangladesh to have signed the UN's Commitment to Sustainable Practices of Higher Education Institutions.[5] According to the SCOPUS indexed research publications in 2019, Daffodil International University has been positioned 4th among all universities and 1st among all private universities in Bangladesh.[6]
